Bank Management System
📌 Project Overview

The Bank Management System is a secure and user-friendly digital platform designed to manage banking operations such as account creation, transactions, balance tracking, and customer management. This system helps automate traditional banking processes, making them faster, safer, and more efficient.

It provides real-time access to banking services for both customers and administrators, ensuring better financial management and transparency.

🚀 Problem Statement

Traditional banking systems often involve manual work, slow processes, and limited accessibility. Customers face challenges such as long waiting times, lack of transparency, and limited digital services.

This project aims to provide a modern, secure, and scalable digital banking solution that enhances user experience and simplifies banking operations.

🎯 Objectives

Digitize and automate banking services.

Provide secure and efficient transaction management.

Enable real-time account and balance tracking.

Improve customer experience and accessibility.
